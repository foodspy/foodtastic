THE PASTA STORY

So how is pasta made?

Typically, pasta is a noodle made from an unleavened dough of a durum wheat flour
mixed with water or eggs and formed into sheets or various shapes, then cooked by boiling or baking.
It can also be made with flour from other cereals or grains.

How many types of pasta are there?

too many. The common ones are linguine, penne, fettuccini, spaghetti, elbow macaroni and lasagne

Is spaghetti a pasta?

Yes. Spaghetti is a type of pasta. People usually think that spaghetti is a totally different thing but it's not
